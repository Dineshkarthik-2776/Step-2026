About STEP

The Science and Technology Entrepreneurship Program (STEP) at Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering is designed to nurture the entrepreneurial spirit among students and provide them with the necessary skills and knowledge to transform innovative ideas into successful ventures.

STEP 2021 focuses on creating a platform for students to explore their entrepreneurial potential, learn from industry experts, participate in hands-on workshops, and showcase their ideas through various events and competitions.

Program Features

Innovation Workshops

Interactive sessions focusing on idea generation, design thinking, and creative problem-solving methodologies.

Mentorship Program

One-on-one guidance from industry experts and successful entrepreneurs to help students refine their ideas.

Startup Competitions

Opportunities to pitch ideas, win prizes, and secure funding for promising startup ventures.
